# 📋 Requirements Documentation

## 📌 Project Goal

To analyze retail inventory and sales data in order to identify inefficiencies and improve profitability.

---

## 👥 Stakeholders

* Business Owner
* Inventory Manager
* Sales Team
* Finance Team

---

## 📊 Business Requirements

* Identify loss-making products
* Detect slow-moving inventory
* Highlight high-demand products
* Analyze regional performance

---

## 🔍 Analytical Requirements

* Aggregate sales and profit data
* Calculate profit margins
* Analyze product performance
* Evaluate trends over time

---

## 📈 Success Criteria

* Clear identification of problem areas
* Actionable insights for decision-making
* Improved understanding of inventory performance
