# 📊 Insights & Findings

## 🔴 Profitability Insights

* Some products are consistently generating losses
* Certain categories have low profit margins despite high sales

---

## 🟡 Inventory Insights

* Several products show very low sales volume (dead stock risk)
* A small number of products drive the majority of sales

---

## 📍 Regional Insights

* Some regions outperform others in both sales and profit
* Underperforming regions may require strategic focus

---

## 📈 Trend Insights

* Sales and profit vary over time
* Possible seasonal patterns observed

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Remove or reprice loss-making products
* Optimize stock levels based on demand
* Focus on high-performing regions
* Improve planning using data-driven insights
